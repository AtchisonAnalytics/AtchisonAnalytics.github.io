INSIDERS Investment Committee

Meeting 1: 19 April 2024


1. REFERENCE PORTFOLIO BACKGROUND

A reference portfolio is proposed to provide a context and investment policy tangibility for this Investment Committee to communicate investment views.

Assumptions:

  • A risk profile that is consistent with a 70% Growth / 30% Defensive portfolio
  • An investment horizon of at least 7 years
  • The reference portfolio should be relatively easy to implement with limited and infrequent ongoing decision management required
  • Provide a modest number of asset class categories with ranges; that provides a flexible policy framework for more strategic and tactical based decisions to then be made within
  • The Reference Portfolio provides framework for attribution analysis of all subsequent strategic and tactical decisions made on the portfolio

2. ASSET CLASS CATEGORIES

Assumptions:

  • Discussion in this IC is to avoid specific fund manager products, and instead focus on asset allocation and portfolio construction level analysis
  • Initial Ranges are proposed and then tested for historical and forecast expected range of outcomes
  • Iteration may be required
  • The portfolio may include less liquid asset classes strategies, however with the acknowledgement of limitations many clients will have regarding practical implementation

Proposed Major Asset Class Categories:

  • Australian Shares
  • International Shares
  • Real Assets (tangible/physical assets)
  • Alternatives (skill based strategies with modest equity/bond correlation)
  • Fixed Duration (capital return is interest rate sensitive)
  • Floating Rate (income return interest rate sensitive)
  • Cash
